How does this work?

With good read you can create your book shelf by adding the books you have already read. You can as well create a collection of the books you want to read. Goodread allow you, also, to setup a yearly challenge for yourself to finish a certain number of books.

Reviews

Reviews will help you to have an idea about the next book you intend to read or to buy. You can also contribute by giving a review about the books you have read so other poeple can benifit from your experience

What are your friends reading?

Chances are your friends are discussing their favorite (and least favorite) books on Goodreads.
